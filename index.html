Projects

A seasonal & daily storage solution for Gatwick Airport Energy by 100% Green Energy PV Generation

   

The storage integration was made by Lithium Ion as a short buffer and Hydrogen Alkaline electrolysis cell with a PEM fuel cell storage as long term seasonal buffer material with Heat Pump system at peak power of 70 MW electrolyzer and 30 MW fuel cell at energy storage capacity of 55 GWh. The total production was 342 GWh with an electricity & heat demand of 151 GWh & 61 GWh.

Hydrogen & Thermochemical Storage system for family house in Spain with 80 % PV Energy

   

The Assessment has been made with Hydrogen storage using electrocatalysis technologies & Thermochemical energy storage (TCM) technologies. The study used PEM electrolyzer for hydrogen with Alkaline electrolysis fuel cell (Electricity , Electricity to Heat Pump to Heat). The TCM materials involve Na2S salt with storage density of 1.58 GJ/m3.
